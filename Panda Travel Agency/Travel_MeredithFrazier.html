Naoshima, Japan

Naoshima is an island in the Seto Inland Sea of Japan. It is known as an island of art, because it has many pieces of contemporary art and architecture. The most well-known museum on the island is the Benesse Art Site Naoshima which features many pieces specifically designed for the island. The Ando Museum, designed by Tadao Ando, is an architectural masterpiece.


Other Activities

Other places to visit around the island include:

  • Chichu Art Museum
  • Needle Factory
  • Inujima Life Garden
